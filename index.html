About Pure GLOW

Pure GLOW is a Nairobi-based organic beauty brand dedicated to helping people achieve healthy, radiant skin and hair using only pure, natural ingredients. Frustrated by the widespread use of harsh chemicals, misleading “natural” claims, and products that cause more harm than good, we set out to create trustworthy, effective alternatives. Every Pure GLOW product is made with certified organic ingredients; many sourced from local Kenyan farms and is completely free from parabens, sulfates, synthetic fragrances, and animal testing.

Our mission is to make clean, high-quality organic beauty accessible and enjoyable for modern Kenyans and beyond. Through this website, we aim to drive online sales of our carefully crafted hair and body products while building long-term trust by sharing transparent ingredient information, educational content, and real self-care inspiration. At Pure GLOW, beauty means purity, simplicity, and a natural glow you can feel proud of inside and out.
